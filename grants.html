Grants That Reklaim

Reklaim Trust uses micro-grants to respond quickly to real needs. Small, direct, and human-centered — every dollar helps stabilize families, spark new businesses, or cover critical gaps.

Why Micro-Grants Matter

Large programs can take months to approve. By then, people have already lost homes, jobs, or opportunities. Reklaim steps in with micro-grants — **fast, accountable, and transparent**. A $200 grant can cover emergency groceries. A $500 grant can buy tools for a new trade. A $1,000 grant can stabilize rent until bigger help arrives.

How Our Grants Work

1. Identify

Needs are identified by local referrals, case managers, or direct applications from the community.

2. Review

Our team verifies eligibility and ensures funds will directly support housing, workforce, or urgent survival needs.

3. Release

Funds are distributed quickly, with reporting and follow-up built in so donors know their impact is real.
