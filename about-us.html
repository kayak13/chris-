About Reklaim Trust

We are more than a nonprofit. We are a faith-rooted movement to restore dignity, hope, and opportunity in Tulare County. Reklaim was built from lived experience: the ache of hardship, the gaps in support, and the belief that God redeems broken things.

Why We Exist

In Tulare County, families face the perfect storm: low wages, rising housing costs, generational poverty, and limited safety nets. For trauma survivors, single parents, and young people without mentors, the path forward feels impossible.

Reklaim was founded to bridge that gap — combining faith, practical aid, and long-term investment. We don’t believe in “one-and-done” charity. We believe in walking with people until they can rebuild their own homes, careers, and futures.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission: Reklaim Trust exists to restore homes, rebuild lives, and raise leaders through faith-rooted support, workforce training, and micro-grants.

Vision: A Central Valley where no family is left behind after crisis, and every young person has a pathway to faith, stability, and opportunity. We envision neighborhoods where restored homes hold restored families, and where survivors of trauma become leaders who mentor others.

Our Core Values

Faith

We believe true restoration begins with God. Our faith guides every decision and every act of service.

Integrity

We commit to transparency in how we steward money, time, and people’s trust. What we promise, we deliver.

Dignity

We serve without shame. Every person deserves to be seen, respected, and treated with worth.

Stewardship

Every resource entrusted to us is used with care — stretching small dollars into big impact.

Community

We believe restoration happens together. From neighbors to partners, we lift each other up.

Hope

We believe in futures that seem impossible. Our job is to help people see light when all they’ve known is darkness.

Leadership

Reklaim is led by people who have walked through hardship themselves — entrepreneurs, trauma survivors, and faith leaders who know restoration is possible because they’ve lived it.
